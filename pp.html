Chrome Extension Privacy Policy – Holoz

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines the principles that Holoz (the "Developer") follows when handling personal information or related data collected through the use of the Google Chrome extension(s) developed by Holoz (the "Extension").

1. Policy Overview

The Developer recognizes the importance of personal information and is committed to protecting it. The Developer will comply with applicable laws and regulations to properly collect, use, and manage personal information.

2. Scope of Application

This Privacy Policy applies solely to the Extension.

3. Permissions Used

The Extension uses the following Chrome permissions as defined in its manifest.json:

  • storage: To store user settings in the browser.

4. Collection and Use of Personal Information

The Developer does not transmit any personally identifiable information to external servers. All configuration data is stored exclusively in the browser's LocalStorage.

4.1 Method of Collection

A settings form is provided within the Extension. Data entered by the user into this form is saved in LocalStorage.

4.2 Purpose of Use

The saved settings improve user convenience by automatically restoring preferences on subsequent uses.

4.3 Retention Period

LocalStorage data is retained until the Extension is uninstalled.

4.4 Consent to Handling of Personal Information

By installing the Extension, users are deemed to have agreed to this Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies

The Extension does not read any cookies from websites, nor does it set or use any proprietary cookies.

6. Management of Personal Information

The Developer implements the following security measures:

6.1 Ensuring Data Accuracy

The Developer strives to maintain accurate and up-to-date data.

6.2 Security Controls

To prevent data breaches or loss, the Extension does not transmit any data to external servers.

6.3 Third-Party Disclosure

The Developer will not disclose personal information to third parties without user consent. If disclosure is required in the future, prior consent will be obtained.

7. Contact Information

For inquiries regarding the Extension or the handling of personal information, please contact:
info@holoz.co.jp
